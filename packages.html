10 Days Angkor Wat is a Hindu temple complex in Cambodia and is the largest religious monument in the world,

the 12th century.It was built by the Khmer King Suryavarman II in the early 12th century in Yaśodharapura,
the capital of the Khmer Empire, as his state temple and eventual mausoleum.
